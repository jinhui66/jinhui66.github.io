Vlog Internet

2023-11-22

Internet

Hello everyone,

Today, I am excited to talk to you about a force that has affected the way we live, work, and connect with each other – the Internet. In just a few decades, the Internet has become an integral part of our daily lives, reshaping the world in ways we could never have imagined. Let’s explore the magic power of the Internet and how it has impacted our society.

(1.Connectivity and Globalization:)举例论证

The Internet’s most evident impact lies in its ability to connect people globally, breaking down geographical barriers. Social media platforms, messaging apps, and video conferencing tools have become essential mediums for communication, enabling instant connections across continents and countries. This interconnected method has not only changed personal relationships but has also transformed the business landscape. Companies can now collaborate seamlessly with partners and customers worldwide, fostering(培育) a global village where ideas, cultures, and commerce converge.

What’s more, the Internet has played a vital role in shaping a wider global society. Through online platforms, individuals from a variety of backgrounds can share experiences, perspectives, and ideas.
The example is the rise of remote work as well as students facilitated(被促进) by video conferencing tools such as ZhuMu and Tencent Conference. This shift has allowed us to immerse ourselves into a global talent pool. And it not only benefits convenience but also contributes to a more various and inclusive study environment.

(2. Access to Information:)优劣分析,对比？

One of the Internet’s most significant contributions is that it has changed the method of access to information. Search engines like Google provide individuals with plenty of access to knowledge, allowing anyone with an internet connection to learn about almost anything. Take me as an example, when final exams are coming, I could review online, which has a more efficient assistance for us to get a high score.

However, when it comes the challenge of these numerous information on the internet, everyone should have a basic self-control, which means we should be equipped with the ability to distinguish the authenticity and quality of information.

(3. Economic Impact:) 数据论证

The Internet has revolutionized the way we conduct business, creating countless economic value and improving our living standard. Now, let me prove it.
It is known that online shopping is a trend in the world.This is because the internet has make shopping more convenient, promoting consumers to purchase their products without going outside. The would has witnessed the rapid growth of GDP in the past decades years. For example, China’s GDP increased nearly 50 times, from 2.72 trillion yuan in 1992 to 121.02 trillion yuan in 2023.

Besides, the internet industry has been popular in China since 1998. So we can see from it, it was internet which promoting the unbelievable growth of GDP.
That’s why I call it enhanced the development in China.

(Conclusion:)

In summary, the Internet has transformed our world in every aspect, touching every corner of our lives. Its impact is undeniable, from the way we communicate and access information to how we conduct business. As we continue to embrace the digital age, let us do so with a sense of responsibility, recognizing the Internet’s potential to create positive change and shape a better future for us all.

Thank you for your time.
